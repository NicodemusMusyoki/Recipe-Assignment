Tenepade(Olive spread)

This is really simple dish to prepare and it's always a big hit at parties. My father recommends:

Make this the night before so the flavours have time to blend. Just bring it up to the room temperature before
you serve it, In the winter, try servingit warm.

Ingredients

  • 8oz. jar sundried tomatoes 2 large garlic cloces
  • 2/3c. kalamata olives
  • 1 t, capers

    • Instructions

    Combine tomatoes and garlic in a food processor. Blend until as smooth as possible.

    Add capers and olives. Pulse the motor a few times until they are incorporated, but still retain some texture.

    Serve in thin toast rounds with goat cheese and fresh basil garnish(optional).
